Project : S.A.V

Project SAV (Service apres vente)is a collaborative effort developed in Spring Boot, integrating Spring Security and Thymeleaf, designed for efficient management of client calls and technical interventions, with a primary focus on French language support and robust security measures.

Visit GitHub repo

Project Overview

This collaborative project, primarily conducted in French to align with our academic studies, is a testament to our commitment to implementing a robust system for managing client calls and technical interventions. Leveraging leading-edge technologies like Spring Boot, Spring Security, and Thymeleaf, we've meticulously crafted an application tailored to address the intricate demands of our scenario.

Our comprehensive solution encompasses features such as efficient management of client calls and dossiers, seamless handling of technical interventions from initiation to closure, and a meticulously designed role-based access control system to ensure secure access and safeguard sensitive data. With multi-language support at its core, prioritizing French to resonate with our academic context, our project reflects not just technical prowess but also a keen understanding of contextual requirements and a dedication to excellence in application development and security implementation.
